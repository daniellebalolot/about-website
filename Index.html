OUR PURPOSE

OF MAKING THIS WEBSITE



COME AND VISIT ZAMBOANGA CITY!

The purpose of developing this website is mainly to

raise awareness on the facts and history of the city Zamboanga.

This website also aims to encourage more

people to visit the city Zamboanga by giving details about

the tourist spots they could visit. Lastly

this serves to help tourists plan their

vacation here in Zamboanga City.
